Marinated Vegetable Salad

Ingredients

  • Mixed Vegetables
  • Apple Cider Vinegar
  • Salt and Pepper

Instructions

  1. Mix vegetables in a large bowl and season with salt and pepper.
  2. Measure 1 TBSP of apple cider vinegar and add to the bowl. Stir well.
  3. Marinate in refrigerator for 24 hours. Enjoy!
